YWCA Food Pantry Inventory

How to use this site
1) Click "Update Inventory" to add or remove stock.
2) Enter the Date, the Item Name, and the Quantity. Use the Add button to increase stock or Subtract to remove stock.
3) After the update completes you can view the current levels under "Current Inventory."
All changes are saved to the server so inventory remains up to date for your team.
